1. GAME OVERVIEW
Title

Chronicles of Aetherion

Genre

Steampunk • Dark Fantasy • Industrial Civilization Simulator • Political Sandbox • Economic Simulation • Tactical RPG • Dynamic AI Sandbox • Persistent World Simulator

Core Experience

Chronicles of Aetherion is a massive fully simulated industrial world where:

AI factions evolve independently
NPCs live complete lives
Economies fluctuate dynamically
Wars happen naturally
Cities grow or collapse
Companies compete
Environments permanently change
Magic corrupts reality
Political systems evolve
History writes itself

The player is NOT the center of the world.

The world continues evolving even without player interaction.
